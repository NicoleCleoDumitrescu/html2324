WHO WE ARE

We are a couture confectionery workshop based in Bucharest, dedicated to crafting the most exquisite cakes, sweets, and tarts for moments of indulgence or special events. Recognized as the premier provider of confections for events, our workshop produces cakes and pastries for weddings, baptisms, and various private and corporate occasions. We also offer candy bar decoration services. All of this comes packaged with personalized consultancy provided by our expert team. Our mission is to bring your most desired cakes, ice cream, and sweets to life in a natural and artisanal manner. We provide you with recipes to recreate them at home—unique recipes with perfectly balanced flavors and textures. These creations are crafted by master pastry chefs using natural ingredients and a blend of artisanal techniques and the latest innovations in haute pâtisserie.
